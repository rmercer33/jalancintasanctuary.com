There are many teachings, many teachers, many pathways. Few give focus to the lived reality of the teachings, the place where “the rubber hits the road.”

We are calling this the Way of Love: not a new message, but the practical and focused application of precious teachings of embodiment that already exist. You can read more about this on the teachings page.

Whatever arises, love that.

Matt Kahn

Jalan Cinta is devoted to the embodiment of Love in all our interactions, day to day and moment to moment, including business, organisational, community, and interpersonal activities… We are devoted to waking up – to embodying Christ – to fully embody the Divine (whatever name you may use to refer to it), here and now, in flesh, in the body: “down and in” with all its apparent contradictions.

Only Love is Real is the calling card. Whatever arises in this moment, we are dedicated to bringing Love to that, to reveal its gems of growth and insight.

We hope you will join with us:

  •   to grow, to heal, to dissolve the limits of awakening
  •   to envision and create a planet healed and humanity at peace
  •   to share what we are truly, whatever the surface appearances – radical, ecstatic Spirit!

Let every step be a meditation on gratitude. Let every breath be an act of receiving and giving the mystery of Grace.

Jayem

Children are not lumps of clay and we are not sculptors. Children are seedlings and we are gardeners.

Richard Eyre

Love, and give all things complete freedom to be and to do as they will.

Jeshua

A radical transformation of humanity is happening.

All over the Earth people are waking up – consciousness is waking up and remembering its Oneness. Another way to say the same is “Love is blossoming in form” – and we are each that Love and that form, bodies walking the Earth as vehicles of Soul.

You are the one that comes to bring Light to this world.

You are infinite. You are eternal.

You are the beauty. You are the beauty of every ray of Light that dances a thousand diamonds across the waters of this planet. You are the beauty and the light of every star.

You are the vastness and the silence and the power of space in which all things have arisen in your little physical universe.

You are the brightest of colors and the deepest of tones.

You are the perfect melody. You are the song, the instrument and its composer, and you are the one that delights in the song.

Jeshua

We are all part of this radical transformation, birthing as the fullness of real humanity. Can there be a greater adventure? How deeply are we willing to dive?
