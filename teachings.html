There are many pathways. The focus of Jalan Cinta’s spirituality is the path of embodiment – “down and in” rather than “up and out” – the teachings that offer an invitation to a transformed life, surrendered in this moment to ife, to Love, to the Divine, whatever you may choose to call it.

To live authentically. To live as Love. What does that mean?

Our friends and visitors draw inspiration from teachers of genuine inner transformation in the grounded, everyday world, including Way of Mastery – as offered through Jayem, A Course in Miracles, the work of Byron Katie, Cheri Huber, Eckhart Tolle, Paul Tuttle – and others. This is the work of turning the pointed finger back to oneself and taking responsibility. Of true forgiveness. Of learning to accept and love the body. Of seeing Christ in everyone. Of loving what is arising now. Of seeing the innocence and play of creation. Of gratitude for it all!

Jalan Cinta will develop a series of signature courses and other activities and events, both onsite and online, with inherently practical content. The focus will be the everyday reality of living as Love.

And that focus is also brought to everything at the Sanctuary: how it is operated, how decisions are made, how we each as individuals – residents, visitors, staff (and the Sanctuary’s pet rabbit) – interact with each other. The place where “the rubber hits the road” is the place of greatest growth! This is what is being created at the Sanctuary: a place where we are each other’s teacher as we learn together to live as Love. Gritty. Real. And sometimes not pretty as the defences against our humanity and our Oneness are let go.

There is only one goal.

To wake up. To wake up from the dream of egoic consciousness into the reality of Oneness, so that the Divine can live “where once I thought I lived.” In other words, to incarnate the Divine.

God wants you! Life wants you! – fully, wholly, embracing “what is” as it unfolds itself. Will you come and play?
