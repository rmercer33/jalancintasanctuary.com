Our Studio Rooms include all of the amenities you would need for a longer term stay at the Sanctuary.

There will be one block of six Studios available (3 upstairs and 3 downstairs). These rooms have been carefully designed to ensure you maximum privacy within your own room.

Some variations are available in the layout of these rooms and Sanctuary Leaseholder who sign on before the building process begins will be able to choose the configuration they prefer.

Our furniture package includes a large desk for creative projects. They have a generous deck and large sliding or folding doors to allow the indoor-outdoor feel that Bali is famous for.

The design and décor has a modern feel – light and airy, simple and elegant with splashes of colour introduced in artwork and textiles. The sacred is consciously brought into the Villa with an altar space, and breathing/yoga floor space.

The Studio rooms will be leaseheld, having full or part-time occupancy, along with no occupancy making them available for visitor accommodation managed by us on your behalf.

Find out more about becoming a Sanctuary Leaseholder.
