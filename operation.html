Our desire is to establish a structure of management that provides transparency, is inclusive, ensures longevity and invites universal wisdom into the decision making process.

Way of Love PMA

Way of Love PMA is a company formed in Indonesia, which owns and operates Jalan Cinta Sanctuary and all associated programs. It will be run by a small group of executives. Specific limitations with Indonesian regulations prevent it from being registered as a Not-For-Profit entity, it will effectively operate as such by investing all profits in service to the healing, awakening and evolution of humanity, through a variety of projects both large and small.

Sanctuary Leaseholders

“Sanctuary Leaseholders” is the term we are using to describe those who choose to join with Way of Love PMA by investing in a long-term lease on one or more of the Villas or Studios built within the Jalan Cinta Sanctuary grounds. This will form a major portion of the Sanctuary funding – find out more about becoming a Sanctuary Leaseholder.

The Council

A Council of Equals, comprising 9 members, will be responsible for the operational decision-making of Way of Love PMA and Jalan Cinta Sanctuary. Each member brings his or her own experience and direct connection to Source in support and service of the Sanctuary.

The formal Council will be appointed when the Sanctuary opens. Council members will go through a nomination and ratification process. All council positions are on a voluntary basis.

Of key importance here is that the Council’s role in decision-making is to come into a resonant field: decisions are made from this place. When truth is spoken, everyone can feel its essence.

The Sages

There are those who have journeyed through life and have deep experience with different areas of expertise – the Wise Ones, our elders and sages – those who can provide support, guidance and share their wisdom.

In recognition of this, our Sages will be appointed by mutual agreement of the Council. They will be approached for their advice, wisdom and experience to assist in resolution of any issues that may arise.
