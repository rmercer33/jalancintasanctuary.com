The physical space

What will it look like? A place of simplicity, elegance and beauty, created in the Balinese style, emerging from the jungle and nestled among native forest trees. A living reflection of Love on Earth.

What will it feel like? A palpably sacred space of peace, a place of healing and quiet joy in nature and companionship… A living emanation of Love on Earth.

What will it sound like? A place of aliveness, with the rushing of the river in the ravine below, of sacred chant and secular dance, of song and laughter… A living sonic vibration of Love on Earth.

What is it for? (as best we know it)

The intention and heartfelt desire is the creation of Jalan Cinta Sanctuary as a living beacon of Love in the world…

… dedicated to the surrendered simplicity of Love in action, the daily lived reality of Love – chosen consciously as individuals and collectively

… supporting the awakening of humanity to remember our Oneness and the truth of who we are: radical ecstatic spirit!

It is also intended as a space for creativity and immersion in connection to the Divine and to Mother Earth, where who-knows-what projects may be birthed for the good of humanity and the glory of That One!

In essence: a place where you can come and join with like-minded and like-hearted friends: to grow, to play, to participate, to experience, to explore, to love, to transform – and to embody the essence of a genuine spirituality.

Come and play!
