The decision to invest in one of the 20 beautiful Villas at Jalan Cinta Sanctuary is a choice that can transform your life and significantly contribute to the transformation of countless others.

The Villas are gorgeous architect-designed, self-contained apartments with either spectacular views or your own private courtyard. And as a “Sanctuary Leaseholder” you will enjoy the daily bliss of a living, breathing, conscious community dedicated to Love.

Overview

The purchase price includes three components: the cost of your Villa, the land, and a contribution to the common facilities.

You have many options for enjoying the benefits of your Villa. You might choose to live at the Sanctuary full time or part time, using it as home base to travel the world, staying for however many months a year that suits you. Or simply consider it as an investment for your own financial security and the awakening of humanity.

Way of Love PMA will manage your Villa for you when you are away from the Sanctuary, – renting it to long-term residents and/or adding it to the accommodation letting pool available for workshop participants and other visitors.

We expect a healthy net return of between 5% and 10%, possibly even more as the Sanctuary gains a worldwide reputation. But please note: this invitation is not for anyone who is simply looking for a way to “make money.” A key factor in joining as a Sanctuary Leaseholder will be your resonance with the vision of what is being birthed here and a desire to join in conscious community.

Please contact us to find out more about the Villa management package. Contact details are at the foot of this page.

Purchase price details

Please note: All financial sums are quoted in USD

The standard Sanctuary Villa lease purchase price is $120,000 Deposit $12,000

 For a limited time we are offering the Villas for $100,000.

To secure this price you will need to pay $50,000 by 1 October 2015.

The reason we are making this offer is because a second payment for the land is due on that date.

A full furniture package is available for $5000. The furniture package is required if you intend to rent your Villa as part of the letting pool. Details of the furniture package are available.

The payment of your deposit will secure the Villa of your choosing from those available. We will discuss with you in detail, one-on-one the relative points of the options available, to help you make your choice.

From our market research we believe the Villas are priced well below comparable Villas for sale in Bali and therefore should be able to achieve a strong resale value.

In Bali most properties are leased from a Balinese owner because of legal restrictions on ownership of land. Way of Love PMA has secured a lease until 1st Jul 2039, with the option for another 25 years. Your Villa will be leased from Way of Love PMA for 24 years initially.

To cover the cost of the day-to-day operating expenses there is an annual Sanctuary Lease Management Fee of $3,000. This covers all maintenance for the first five years, sewerage, electricity, tap water, garbage collection and gas. It also entitles you to a Sanctuary pass, which will provide you with entry to all the general daily and weekly activities including yoga, breathing, chanting, movies and talks.
